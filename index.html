Getting You to Your Destination
Traffic Free. Pollution Free.

Benzinati was founded on three pillars: a promise to deliver excellence to our community, a passion to introduce innovative solutions to global challenges and a commitment to give our customers all the data they need to make informed and educated decisions.
Our team develops tech-based products that help our customers reduce their car expenses, environmental fingerprint and time spent in traffic.

Values

Excellence

From research to execution, we commit to excellence by recruiting top talent, implementing innovative solutions and delivering best value to our community.

Accountability

Our top priority is to act responsibly and always do what is right to our team, customers, partners and planet.

Integrity

To reach our goals, we adopt a direct, transparent and honest approach by encouraging open discussion and giving our customers all data required to make informed and educated decisions.
